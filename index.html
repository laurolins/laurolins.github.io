Research Interests
•    Visualization Systems
•    Metric Spaces for "Complex Objects"
•    Information Visualization
•    Combinatorial Optimization
•    Graph Drawing

I am currently working on two main projects. The first one is an information visualization system called DEFOG: Data Exploration with Faces Objects and Groups. DEFOG is an environment to visually and programmatically explore data. It is a general and flexible tool that is becoming the platform for a set of different projects we are currently working on in our group and in collaboration with other institutions (cancer risk datasets, biochemical pathways, mice experiments). The second project I am working on is a formal model that generalizes many distance metrics for graphs. Actually the model enables us to show that concepts of "edit distance" and "intersection" are equivalent for a rich set of objects (e.g. graphs whose vertices and edges are labeled with other graphs).
